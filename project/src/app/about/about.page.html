About

Cibo is an app that provides a platform for users to exchange information about the food they own and let them share the food with friends for pick up.

We crafted the app with the Sustainable Development Goals in mind in attempt to reduce the amount of food waste generated, and create a sustainable and responsible consumption cycle.
